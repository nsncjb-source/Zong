HOW IT WORKS

Detect, verify, respond

No polling, no delay loop — ZONG reacts to the same audit-log event Discord itself generates the instant something happens.

01 / DETECT

Pattern recognition

Mass channel deletions, mass bans, or role/permission stripping within a short window trips the detector — the real signature of a nuke, not a guess.

02 / VERIFY

Exemption check

Whitelisted members, trusted roles, and the server owner are never auto-punished — false positives are checked out before any action is taken.

03 / RESPOND

Automatic action

Kick, ban, or quarantine — whichever you've configured — fires immediately, with a live alert to your chosen channel.
